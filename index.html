A Dataset for Assessing Worker Activities in Industrial Settings

This dataset captures detailed sensor readings from wearable devices to assess and classify worker activities in real-world industrial environments. It includes annotated accelerometer and gyroscope data, enabling research in activity recognition, safety monitoring, and human-centred industrial automation. Ideal for machine learning and IoT applications focused on enhancing workplace efficiency and safety.
